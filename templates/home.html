What's going on here?

This is a project that uses collaborative filtering to find the best board games for you to try based on how you -- and other people like you -- have rated thousands of games on BoardGameGeek.com.

I created this recommender as my capstone project for a Data Science training program at Galvanize

I'm rolling out the recommender for a short beta test ahead of my final presentation. I pledge all proceeds raised during this beta period to Extra Life, a game-focussed charity that raises money for Children's Miracle Network Hospitals.

If you like what I'm doing here (or if you think there's something I could do better) please let me know. You can find a feedback form on the recommendations page.
